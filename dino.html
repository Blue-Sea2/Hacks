Dino Game Hack

To open the dino game go to chrome://dino in the Google Chrome browser.
Then open the console and paste 

var original = Runner.prototype.gameOver; Runner.prototype.gameOver = function(){}
You should now be invincible.
To stop being invincible, paste 
Runner.prototype.gameOver = original
